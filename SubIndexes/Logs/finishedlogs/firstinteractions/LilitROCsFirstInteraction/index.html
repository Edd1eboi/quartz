Lilit and Rosie’s First Interaction.

Participants: Nora , RO-C and Lilit .

Place: Halpha , Central City , by the café.

RO-C : “Hiya Nora !!”

Nora sees RO-C approaching: “Hey Ross~”

^00bbb3

Nora : “How’s it going?”

RO-C : “R-Ross!?! Why that name??” she says, replying in protest.

Nora : “Rose- Ross, similar enough.”

Nora : “Gender is a social construct that doesn’t have any beneficial purpose to society–”

RO-C : “N-No its not!! Ross is a boy’s name!” Rosie says, fuming.

Nora : “Fine I’ll call you Rosie.” She said, noticing her fumes.

Rosie puffs her cheeks out in annoyance.

Nora : “You’ll learn about this later in school.” Nora chuckled.

RO-C : “Well I happen to like my name” She said with pride and a smile.

Nora : “That’s fortunate~”

RO-C : “Hehe~ Anyways, I came in for something to eat. What’s on the menu?”

Nora : “Ah, right~ I don’t work at the cafe anymore.”

RO-C : “Oh really? That’s too bad….”

Nora : “Yeah, I had a falling ou- Oh?”

Rosie thought she saw something moving in the bushes and turns to look.

RO-C : “Hmm?!”

Nora approached the bush: “Who resides here?”

Lilit : “Blub blub”

RO-C : “Umm… hello…??”

Nora : “I don’t think fishes are meant for bushes.” She said, pulling Lilit ’s fin-ears.

RO-C : “Ah!”

Lilit : “What the hell, skunk girl”

Nora : “That’s my spot, dummy.”

Lilit : “I know. That’s why I took it” Lilit : “It smells nice.”

Nora : “There are literally- Oh.”

RO-C : “Umm… hello there.”

Nora : “Oh right, have you met Lilit , Rosie?”

RO-C : “No… this is the first time I’ve met her.”

Nora : She whispers to Rosie: “She’s a little crazy, but that’s okay~”

RO-C : “I’m Rosie! Rosie Clements! Who might you be?”

Lilit : “Hello, little child.”

Lilit : “I’m Lady Lilit of Atlantis” Lilit : “You can call me Lilit though.”

RO-C : “Oh! Nice to meet you Ms. Lilit .”

Lilit : “Miss.. Finally being addressed with the respect I deserve” Lilit : “Thank you little one.”

Nora : “Shut up Lily.”

Lilit : “Don’t be crude Nora , you don’t want to scare your new friend away”

Nora : “I’ve known Rosie for a while actually~”

RO-C : “Yep! Yep!” Rosie Nodded.

Lilit : “Well, good to see you hang out with someone your age for once.”

Nora : “Oh shut it, fish lady.”

RO-C : “So Lilit … since you’re a lady… does that mean you’re royalty?”

Lilit : “Yes, somewhere I am royalty, not here though really.” Lilit : “At least, nobody here acknowledges my status. Annoyingly enough…”

Nora : “She’s a Meteorn like me.”

RO-C : “Oh, I’m sorry to hear that… it must be hard knowing you’re so far from home…”

Lilit : “Yes, not only is it far away from home, but I’m also stuck on land”

Nora : “There’s water like 3 meters from where you’re standing.”

RO-C : “I don’t think she means that kind of water Norraa~”

Nora : “Not my fault.” Nora snorts out.

Lilit : “I mean. The sea.”

Nora : “That’s like a kilometer out–”

RO-C : “Yeah! There’s plenty of water to the west or south of here!”

Nora : “Yeah, even in the north-east.”

Lilit : “Yeah but there’s no sea people in there.” Lilit : “and no buildings. or roads.” Lilit : “It’s just water. Really weird.”

RO-C : “Is that what Atlantis is like?”

Lilit : “Yes. It’s a city like this, but underwater.” Lilit : “I don’t remember that much else about it though… Like most people here”

Nora : “Did you walk around in Atlantis?”

RO-C : “Yeah! Or did you swim around? Maybe even had your own cars?” She asks, with wonder in her eyes.

Lilit : “Either works. Depends on what you’re feeling.” Lilit : “We had cars, kinda, more like pods. But I crashed like a bunch of my dads pods so he banned me from using them” Lilit : “I think anyways, kinda hazy memory. Maybe it was just one.”

RO-C : “Maybe it was just the one before you ended up on Halpha ?”

Nora : “We can’t really know, can we?”

RO-C : “I guess not…”

Nora : “I, along with Lilit , can’t remember much from before, only glimpses of the past.”

Lilit : “Yeah. I don’t know the veracity of anything I might think I remember. It kinda sucks. But, at least I’m not burdened by my past.”

RO-C : “So it’s kind of like having a clean slate to work with then!”

Lilit : “Exactly. I suppose it’s sad in a way, not remembering your family wholly, but at least you can be a whole new person.”

Nora : “Who knows, I might’ve been a megalomaniac~”

Lilit : “You’re just a little kid. You were probably eating dirt or something.”

RO-C : “You certainly have the capacity for it Nora ~” Rosie chides playfully

Nora : “Oh- Well- You can’t bully me like this.”

Lilit : She imitates Nora ’s voice: “Om-nom-nom, dirt so yummy, Nora like-y.”

Nora : “I will grind dirt in your eyes, old lady.” Nora said, picking up a handful of dirt menacingly.

RO-C : Rosie gigles at Lilit ’s immitation “Hehe… I don’t think Nora ’s that bad…”

Nora : “I wouldn’t just eat –any– old dirt, it’d have to be of the finest quality.”

Lilit pulls out her sunglasses “Try it, squirrel.”

RO-C : “Umm… maybe the dirt would be put to better use for planting flowers instead?”

Nora puts on the glasses: “Is this what you wanted?”

Nora : “Perhaps, if you’re a lowly dirt-consumer, but I, Nora Honora , know the fine art of eating dirt.”

RO-C : “You look nice with the- w-wait what?” Rosie stuttered at Nora ’s dirt eating remark.

Nora : “What?”

Lilit pulls out another pair of glasses and puts them on: “Yes.”

Nora : “Why do you have so many glasses?”

Lilit : “I had a hard time adapting to life above the sea, it was hard to see things - so I got a lot of glasses.” Lilit : “I’m used to it now though.”

Nora takes the glasses off and casts them to Lilit : “That’s fair enough.”

Lilit Puts all the glasses back in her pockets.

RO-C : “So Lilit , how old are you if you remember?”

Lilit : “I believe one or two thousand years, I’m not sure though.” Lilit : “I believe we fish people age much slower than you warm blooded folks.”

Nora : “Gosh, so you’re eternally annoying?”

Lilit : “I will haunt you forevaaaa~” Lilit : “Or, until you die, and I’m still alive.”

Nora : “That’s kind of sad when you think about it.”

Lilit : “Yeah, so make sure you don’t annoy me too much so I can remember you fondly.” Lilit : “Because I will remember you forevaaaaaaa~”

Nora : “If you say you’re around two thousand years old then that means I’ve lived for less than a percent of your lifespan.”

Lilit : “Anyways, how old are you, little Rosie”

RO-C : “I’m thirteen!”

Lilit : “That’s pretty close to Nora ’s age. Which is good. There’s not an abundance of kids around here.”

Nora : “Close-?! That’s not close!”

Lilit : “I mean.. From my perspective…”

Nora : “fRoM mY PeRsPEcTive- Shush.” Nora said mockingly.

RO-C : “Huh…. I guess thirteen and seventeen would be pretty close to someone who can live for a thousand years…”

Lilit : “I literally don’t even remember being either of your ages. It was that long ago.”

Nora : “You don’t remember anything.”

Lilit : “I remember what I did to- uh nevermind.”

Nora : “To?”

Lilit : “ANYWAYS.”

Nora : “No– I wanna hear this.”

Lilit : “I need to go.. uh..-”

Nora : “You’re not going anywhere.”

Lilit : “I promised to water– Someone’s uh…- Fish. So yeah, bye. Lilit hastily stands up and runs away.

Nora : “God damn it.”

RO-C : “Oh! Bye Ms. ** Lilit **t”

RO-C : “I guess she really had to leave…”

Nora : “She’s hiding something.” Nora : “She never told me how she got those legs either.”

RO-C : “Why? Is that not normal for her?”

Nora : “Apparently not, and when I asked her she said that I don’t want to know what she did to get those legs.”

RO-C : “Sounds like the hook for a fairy tale…” or a horror story.”

Nora : “Yeah- Oh, hey there again.” Nora saw Lilit running back.

RO-C : “Oh! Welcome back Ms. Lilit !”

Lilit : “Hello little babies.”

Nora : “Now tell me how you got ‘em legs.”

Lilit : “Alright I’m leaving again.”

Nora : “No no!”

RO-C : “It’s okay! You don’t have to if it’s private to you!” Rosie said, reassuringly.

Nora : “It’s suspicious…”

RO-C : “It might be painful to recall too…”

Lilit : “Exactly, Rosie. Glad someone around here taught you good manners.”

Nora menancingly brings up the dirt from the ground again: “It best be something personal…”

RO-C : Rosie smiles at Lilit ’s praise “My mom taught me this and some of the shows I watch”

Lilit : “Oh, what shows? My little photon? I love that show.”

Lilit side-eyes Nora : “Found a snack, I see.”

Nora : “Shut.”

RO-C : “Yeah! It’s a great show on friendship I think!”

Nora : “Mhm.”

Lilit : “That’s one good thing about this place. All the good shows.”

Nora : “I take it that there weren’t many shows in Atlantis.”

Lilit : “No shows at all - no visiphone or bisiphone or whatever you kids call it.”

Rosie’s MAG began ringing, interrupting the conversation.

Nora : “Oh, what’s that?”

RO-C : “Uh oh!”

Rosie then open’s up the MAG’s projected keyboard and starts typing something in.

Nora : “Hm?!”

RO-C : “Hey… sorry but I have to go soon! I promised to finish up an errand for my parents and they’re asking where I’m at.”

Lilit : “So responsible of you, see you later little Rosie.”

Nora : “I see, it was nice catching up with you~”

RO-C : “I’ll be back here later if I get the okay from my parents.”

Nora : “Alright!~”

RO-C : “Bye now! Nice meeting you Lilit !”

Nora : “Bye, little lady!” she said, waving.

Lilit : “Nice meeting you little one!”

Nora and Lilit remained sitting in the grass for a few moments, before parting aswell.
