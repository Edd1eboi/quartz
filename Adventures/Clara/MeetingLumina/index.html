(ooc: 2021-10-07)

Clara walks up to a pleasant looking lady who seems to be in thought.

Clara : “Hey there…”

Lumina : “You need something?”

Clara : “I- uh… I don’t know.”

Lumina : “You don’t know. Hmmph. You’re not ‘new’ are you?”

Clara : “Uhm, define new?”

Lumina : “Nevermind.” Lumina said, “Hello to you too.”

Clara : She bowed lightly: “Good to meet you, miss…?”

Lumina : “ Lumina . Science division.”

Clara : “ Lumina ?~ That’s a nice name.”

Lumina : “Hmm… Yours?”

Clara : “My name’s Clara , ms. Lumina .”

Lumina : “ Clara , that too is a nice name.” Lumina said, pulling up her console.

Clara : “Oh? Thank you.” She noticed the console and proceeded to ask: “What are you doing there?”

Clara : She walked up behind Lumina and peered over her shoulder: “Hm.”

Lumina : “Nothing really.” Lumina said, “Just looking over relevant data.”

Lumina : It was data from the last urgent quest. She was compiling numbers.

Clara : “Right, you were with the science division.” She look confused at the data: “What are you analyzing?”

Lumina : “Combat records, so we can keep track of the opposition’s numbers.”

Lumina : “It allows us to prepare ourselves next time.”

Clara : “I see. Anything conclusive?”

Lumina : “We’re seeing more Pettax grade DOLLS going after the mining rigs.”

Lumina : “It’s been steadily growing, the average that is.”

Clara : “What about the median?” She responded slightly teasingly.

Lumina : “You’ll see it all in a viewscreen somewhere.”

Clara : “Hm, okay.” Suddenly her stomach audibly growled and nervously she said: “You don’t happen to know where I could get a meal?”

Lumina : “… How long have you lived in this city?” Lumina asked Clara .

Clara : “Uh, well, I got here today.”

Lumina : “So you ARE new. Are you registered with ARKS?”

Clara : “Yeah! I fought some terrifying three-headed monster.”

Lumina : “You’re new and you went against a Nex? Not TOO bad. Come on.” Lumina : said, dismissing her console and walking.

Clara : “Oh! Okay.”

The two walked over to the café.

Lumina : “This is the café. You can get something to eat here.”

Clara : “Ah I see, would you perhaps like to join me for a meal? First day and all.” She anxiously leered over at the bar-disk.

Lumina : “… … I suppose so.” Lumina said, walking under the disk-like roof with a sigh.

Lumina : Lumina looked to Clara . “What do you want?”

Clara : “Some sort of food.” She responded anxiously, at a loss of words.

Clara : Clara stood behind Lumina :, almost hiding from the bar-maid’s glare: “Could you, uh.. possibly order for me?”

Clara : She ran off to hide behind a tree: “You’ve, uh.. got this…!”

Lumina : “What the Hell are…” Lumina frowned at the woman, and scoffed. “Two shrimp spring roll plates and two teas.”

Clara : The bar-maid responds: “Of course, take a seat over there.”

Lumina : “Thanks.” she moved to sit.

Clara : Clara : carefully moved to the table where Lumina : was sat.

Clara : “T..- thanks… for ordering…”

Lumina : “Sit here”

Clara : “O- okay.” Clara : She took a seat, gracefully.

Lumina : “… What got into you? Why the sudden shyness?”

Clara : “Uh, ergh… well, I just get really like, scared almost, talking to people.”

Lumina : “… You talked to me just fine earlier.”

Clara : “W- well, you looked nice to talk to, you get what I mean?”

Lumina : “… You felt comfortable around me due to my appearance?”

Clara : “Sort of, more-so your… aura? I don’t know how to explain it.”

Lumina : “Aura. Interesting.” Lumina said, “Perhaps it’s my photon affinity.”

Clara : “Photon affinity?”

Lumina : “Or rather, photon aptitude. Having a high photon aptitude means that I am able to control photons far better than others.” Lumina : “Those with a high photon aptitude can also have this… aura surrounding them.”

Clara : “I assume there’s some sort of ‘but’ coming after that.”

Lumina : “It CAN be dangerous. If I have a higher photon aptitude, but my body is unable to withstand the stress it brings…”

Lumina : “It can be bad.”

Clara : “Guess you’ve gotta be able to control ‘em photons real good, eh…?” She joked, attempting to raise the mood.

Lumina : “I do. My body must be treated like a temple if I am to keep my own photons from burning me from the inside.”

Clara : “I want to say that’s rather dark, though I’d be lying as photons are rather bright.”

Lumina : Pretty soon, their food would come. Each woman got two shrimp spring rolls with lines of peanut sauce drizzled over them. Lumina : They also each got iced tea, sweetened carefully as to not be too overwhelming.

Clara : She awed at the food and drinks that were infront of the two.

Lumina : They could see there were three shrimps on top of the spring rolls, just under the clear wrapping. Lumina took one and bit into it. There was a satisfying crunch to it from the vegetables.

Clara : Clara looked as Lumina ate the roll and brought forth her shaking hand to grab a roll aswell.

Clara : She took a bite and her eyes lit up: “This…”

Clara : “This is amazing.”

Lumina : “It’s good. Don’t worry.” Lumina said. The newman may not realize it, but her voice was also very calming.

Clara : “Mm-hm!” She responded now, having a mouth full of spring rolls.

Lumina : Lumina : shook her head and enjoyed her’s as a respectable pace.

Clara : “STh-is-… ishch… so… gswawhood…” She said and quickly realizing that her mouth was full. She embarrassingly looked down at her plate to avoid eye contact.

Lumina : “Aelio seafood can’t be beat.” Lumina said, finishing one of her rolls.

Clara : “Mhm, though I don’t know if that’s my hunger speaking. Haven’t had food all day.”

Lumina : “You can’t go without eating.” Lumina said. “Eat until you’re full.”

Clara : “It’s been an eventful day, to say the least. Thank you for the food~”

Clara : responded, taking another bite and a sip-

Clara : of the iced tea.

Lumina : “No problem.” Lumina said, getting to her second spring roll. They were rather big, able to fill up small bellies.

Clara : “If you don’t mind me asking, how’d you end up in this place? I assume through the ‘big-space-pod-thing’?

Lumina : “… … Yeah.”

Clara : “…how long have you been here?”

Lumina : “More than a decade. I came here as a child.”

Clara : “Oh man.”

Clara : “Is coming as a child common here?”

Lumina : “It isn’t.”

Clara : “Must’ve been lonely.”

Lumina : “It was.” Lumina said, “But I had people to look after me.”

Lumina : She finished her springrolls, and sipped her tea. That hit the spot.

Clara : “That’s good atleast.” She stated, “How come I can’t remember anything from.. before the crash…?”

Lumina : “That is a question we all ask ourselves. We don’t remember. Most of us don’t. But we intend to find out why.”

Clara : “That’s something thankfully.”

Clara : “If nobody can remember anything it’s most likely not a crash-related head injury.”

Clara : “Must be something with this place - I noticed the almost constructed way everything is.”

Lumina : “That is true. We find this place strange, even if it’s all we ever knew. It implies that we remember someplace different..”

Clara : “Yeah. I’ve had a little time to think about the reasons why I was here while walking between checkpoints and–

Clara : have reasoned that it’s mostly likely some kind of field, perhaps a photon field, that alters the memory of people.”

Clara : “Not sure what kind of civilization that would be in possesion of that kind of technology, but they must be powerful.”

Lumina : “It’s a solid theory. This place DOES seem to be made for us. The training cocoons, the towers, the abundant food.”

Lumina : “But then you have the DOLLs. Our enemies.”

Clara : “Any idea what they are?”

Lumina : “Eternal enemies, training robots, we’re not sure. At the moment all we can do is beat them in an arms race.”

Clara : “How are we supposed to beat an enemy that we don’t know?”

Lumina : “We learn.”

Clara : “I see.” Clara : “I wonder why the civilization before us decided to create a mind-altering field.”

Lumina : “We won’t know for some time.” Lumina : “Are you full?”

Clara : “Yeah! This was great, thank you very much.” Clara : She ate the last roll out of gluttony.

Lumina : “Good. Get used to the people here. You won’t last if you can’t handle speaking to them.”

Clara : “Erhh… could you maybe, by chance, help me with that?”

Lumina : “I suppose if I must.” Lumina : She picked herself up and put a hand to her stomach. She could appreciate a full tummy, the way it felt.

Clara : “You don’t have to if you don’t want to!” She reassured, weakly.

Lumina : “Then again, it is one of my duties as a ground operator.”

Clara : “Oh- alright!”

Lumina : “You’re rather tall, by the way.”

Clara : “As are you~”

Lumina : “Well, if I was shorter, I may have a harder time managing my power.”

Clara : “Maybe it’d scale proportionally?”

Lumina : “You mean I’ll grow bigger?”

Clara : “Oh no, I meant that you’d have less power if you were shorter.”

Lumina : “Ah, well probably not. I know a short girl who’s power rivals mine. She has to run and exercise everyday to keep it under control.”

Clara : “Oh really?”

Lumina : “Yeah. She is wired.” Lumina said, “What about you? Can you feel the energy around you?”

Clara : “I can’t really feel it, it’s more like it’s a pleasant looking energy around certain things or people, like yourself.”

Lumina : “Alright, well, we’ll see how you fair in time. I need to get back to work. If I see you, I’ll help you more.”

Clara : “Thank you very much Miss Lumina ~”

Lumina : “Sure thing, Clara .”

Clara : “Good luck with your analyzing.”

Clara remained at the café whilst Lumina went off to do her business.
